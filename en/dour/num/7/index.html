1And it came to pass in the day that Moses had finished the tabernacle, and set it up, and had anointed and sanctified it with all its vessels, the altar likewise and all the vessels thereof, 2The princes of Israel and the heads of the families, in every tribe, who were the rulers of them who had been numbered, offered 3Their gifts before the Lord, six wagons covered, and twelve oxen. Two princes offered one wagon, and each one an ox, and they offered them before the tabernacle. 4Offered a silver dish weighing one hundred and thirty sides, a silver bowl of seventy sides according to the weight of the sanctuary, both full of flour tempered with oil for a sacrifice: 5And the Lord said to Moses: 6Receive them from them to serve in the ministry of the tabernacle, and thou shalt deliver them to the Levites according to the order of their ministry. 7Moses therefore receiving the wagons and the oxen, delivered them to the Levites. 8Two wagons and four oxen he gave to the sons of Gerson, according to their necessity. 9The other four wagons, and eight oxen he gave to the sons of Merari, according to their offices and service, under the hand of Ithamar the son of Aaron the priest. 10But to the sons of Caath he gave no wagons or oxen: because they serve in the sanctuary and carry their burdens upon their own shoulders. 11And the princes offered for the dedication of the altar on the day when it was anointed, their oblation before the altar. 12And the Lord said to Moses: Let each of the princes one day after another offer their gifts for the dedication of the altar. 13The first day Nahasson the son of Aminadab of the tribe of Juda offered his offering: 14And his offering was a silver dish weighing one hundred and thirty sides, a silver bowl of seventy sides according to the weight of the sanctuary, both full of flour tempered with oil for 15A little mortar of ten sides of gold full of incense: 16An ox of the herd, and a ram, and a lamb of a year old for a holocaust: 17And a buck goat for sin: 18And for the sacrifice of peace offerings, two oxen, five rams, five he goats, five lambs of a year old. These things were offered in the dedication of the altar, when it was anointed. 90And when Moses entered into the tabernacle of the covenant, to consult the oracle, he heard the voice of one speaking to him from the propitiatory, that was over the ark between the two cherubims, and from this place he spoke to him.
