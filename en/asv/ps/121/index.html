Language > English > American Standard Version > Psalms > Chapter 121

1I will lift up mine eyes unto the mountains: From whence shall my help come? 2My help [cometh] from Jehovah, Who made heaven and earth. 3He will not suffer thy foot to be moved: He that keepeth thee will not slumber. 4Behold, he that keepeth Israel Will neither slumber nor sleep. 5Jehovah is thy keeper: Jehovah is thy shade upon thy right hand. 6The sun shall not smite thee by day, Nor the moon by night. 7Jehovah will keep thee from all evil; He will keep thy soul. 8Jehovah will keep thy going out and thy coming in From this time forth and for evermore. Psalm 122 A Song of Ascents; of David.
