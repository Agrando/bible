Language > English > American King James Version > Psalms > Chapter 15

1Lord, who shall abide in your tabernacle? who shall dwell in your holy hill? 2He that walks uprightly, and works righteousness, and speaks the truth in his heart. 3He that backbites not with his tongue, nor does evil to his neighbor, nor takes up a reproach against his neighbor. 4In whose eyes a vile person is contemned; but he honors them that fear the LORD. He that swears to his own hurt, and changes not. 5He that puts not out his money to usury, nor takes reward against the innocent. He that does these things shall never be moved.
