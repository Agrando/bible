Language > English > Geneva Bible > Psalms > Chapter 149

1Praise ye the LORD. Sing unto the LORD a new song, [and] his praise in the congregation of saints. 2Let Israel rejoice in him that made him: let the children of Zion be joyful in their King. 3Let them prayse his Name with the flute: let them sing prayses vnto him with the timbrell and harpe. 4For the Lorde hath pleasure in his people: he will make the meeke glorious by deliuerance. 5Let the saints be joyful in glory: let them sing aloud upon their beds. 6Let the high Actes of God bee in their mouth, and a two edged sword in their hands, 7To execute vengeance upon the heathen, [and] punishments upon the people; 8To bind their kings with chains, and their nobles with fetters of iron; 9To execute upon them the judgment written: this honour have all his saints. Praise ye the LORD.
