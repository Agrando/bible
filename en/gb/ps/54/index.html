Language > English > Geneva Bible > Psalms > Chapter 54

1«To the chief Musician on Neginoth, Maschil, [A Psalm] of David, when the Ziphims came and said to Saul, Doth not David hide himself with us?» Save me, O God, by thy name, and judge me by thy strength. 2O God, heare my prayer: hearken vnto the wordes of my mouth. 3For strangers are risen up against me, and oppressors seek after my soul: they have not set God before them. Selah. 4Behold, God [is] mine helper: the Lord [is] with them that uphold my soul. 5He shall reward evil unto mine enemies: cut them off in thy truth. 6I will freely sacrifice unto thee: I will praise thy name, O LORD; for [it is] good. 7For he hath delivered me out of all trouble: and mine eye hath seen [his desire] upon mine enemies.
