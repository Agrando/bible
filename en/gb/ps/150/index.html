Language > English > Geneva Bible > Psalms > Chapter 150

1Praise ye the LORD. Praise God in his sanctuary: praise him in the firmament of his power. 2Prayse ye him in his mightie Actes: prayse ye him according to his excellent greatnesse. 3Praise him with the sound of the trumpet: praise him with the psaltery and harp. 4Prayse ye him with timbrell and flute: praise ye him with virginales and organs. 5Prayse ye him with sounding cymbales: prayse ye him with high sounding cymbales. 6Let every thing that hath breath praise the LORD. Praise ye the LORD.
