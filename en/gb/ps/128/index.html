Language > English > Geneva Bible > Psalms > Chapter 128

1«A Song of degrees.» Blessed [is] every one that feareth the LORD; that walketh in his ways. 2For thou shalt eat the labour of thine hands: happy [shalt] thou [be], and [it shall be] well with thee. 3Thy wife [shall be] as a fruitful vine by the sides of thine house: thy children like olive plants round about thy table. 4Lo, surely thus shall the man be blessed, that feareth the Lord. 5The LORD shall bless thee out of Zion: and thou shalt see the good of Jerusalem all the days of thy life. 6Yea, thou shalt see thy childrens children, and peace vpon Israel.
