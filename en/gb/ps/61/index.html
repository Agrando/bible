Language > English > Geneva Bible > Psalms > Chapter 61

1To him that excelleth on Neginoth. A Psalme of Dauid. Heare my cry, O God: giue eare vnto my prayer. 2From the end of the earth will I cry unto thee, when my heart is overwhelmed: lead me to the rock [that] is higher than I. 3For thou hast bene mine hope, and a strong tower against the enemie. 4I will dwell in thy Tabernacle for euer, and my trust shall be vnder the couering of thy wings. Selah. 5For thou, O God, hast heard my vows: thou hast given [me] the heritage of those that fear thy name. 6Thou wilt prolong the king's life: [and] his years as many generations. 7He shall abide before God for ever: O prepare mercy and truth, [which] may preserve him. 8So will I alway sing prayse vnto thy Name in performing dayly my vowes.
