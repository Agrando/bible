Language > English > Geneva Bible > Psalms > Chapter 93

1The LORD reigneth, he is clothed with majesty; the LORD is clothed with strength, [wherewith] he hath girded himself: the world also is stablished, that it cannot be moved. 2Thy throne [is] established of old: thou [art] from everlasting. 3The floods have lifted up, O LORD, the floods have lifted up their voice; the floods lift up their waves. 4The waues of ye sea are marueilous through the noyse of many waters, yet the Lorde on High is more mightie. 5Thy testimonies are very sure: holiness becometh thine house, O LORD, for ever.
