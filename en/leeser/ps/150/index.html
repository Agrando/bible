Language > English > Leeser Old Testament > Psalms > Chapter 150

1Hallelujah. Praise ye God in his sanctuary: praise him in the expansion of his power. 2Praise him for his acts of might: praise him according to the abundance of his greatness. 3Praise him with the blowing of the cornet: praise him with the psaltery and harp. 4Praise him with the timbrel and dance: praise him with stringed instruments and pipe. 5Praise him upon the clearringing cymbals: praise him upon the highsounding cymbals. 6Let every thing that hath breath praise the Lord. Hallelujah.
