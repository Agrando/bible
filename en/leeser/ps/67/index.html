Language > English > Leeser Old Testament > Psalms > Chapter 67

1To the chief musician on Neginoth, a psalm or song. May God be gracious unto us, and bless us; may he cause his face to shine upon us. Selah. 2That upon earth men may know thy way, among all nations thy salvation. 3The people will thank thee, O God; the people, all of them together, will thank thee. 4Nations will rejoice and sing for joy: when thou judgest the people righteously, and guidest the nations upon earth. Selah. 5The people will thank thee, O God; the people, all of them together, will thank thee. 6The earth yieldeth her products: yea, God, our own God, bless us. 7God will bless us: and all the ends of the earth shall fear him.
