Language > English > King James 2000 > Psalms > Chapter 54

1To the Chief Musician on neginoth. A maschil, a Psalm of David, when the Ziphites came and said to Saul, Does not David hide himself with us? Save me, O God, by your name, and judge me by your strength. 2Hear my prayer, O God; give ear to the words of my mouth. 3For strangers are risen up against me, and oppressors seek after my soul: they have not set God before them. Selah. 4Behold, God is my helper: the Lord is with them that uphold my soul. 5He shall reward evil unto my enemies: cut them off in your truth. 6I will freely sacrifice unto you: I will praise your name, O LORD; for it is good. 7For he has delivered me out of all trouble: and my eye has seen his desire upon my enemies.
