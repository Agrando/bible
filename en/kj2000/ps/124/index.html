Language > English > King James 2000 > Psalms > Chapter 124

1A song of ascents. Of David. If it had not been the LORD who was on our side, now may Israel say; 2If it had not been the LORD who was on our side, when men rose up against us: 3Then they would have swallowed us up alive, when their wrath was kindled against us: 4Then the waters would have overwhelmed us, the stream would have gone over our soul: 5Then the proud waters would have gone over our soul. 6Blessed be the LORD, who has not given us as a prey to their teeth. 7Our soul is escaped as a bird out of the snare of the fowlers: the snare is broken, and we are escaped. 8Our help is in the name of the LORD, who made heaven and earth.
