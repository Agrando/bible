Language > English > King James 2000 > Psalms > Chapter 67

1To the Chief Musician on neginoth. A Song or Psalm. God be merciful unto us, and bless us; and cause his face to shine upon us; Selah. 2That your way may be known upon earth, your saving health among all nations. 3Let the people praise you, O God; let all the people praise you. 4O let the nations be glad and sing for joy: for you shall judge the people righteously, and govern the nations upon earth. Selah. 5Let the people praise you, O God; let all the people praise you. 6Then shall the earth yield her increase; and God, even our own God, shall bless us. 7God shall bless us; and all the ends of the earth shall fear him.
