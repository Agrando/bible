Language > English > King James 2000 > Psalms > Chapter 121

1A song of ascents. I will lift up my eyes unto the hills, from which comes my help. 2My help comes from the LORD, who made heaven and earth. 3He will not allow your foot to be moved: he that keeps you will not slumber. 4Behold, he that keeps Israel shall neither slumber nor sleep. 5The LORD is your keeper: the LORD is your shade upon your right hand. 6The sun shall not strike you by day, nor the moon by night. 7The LORD shall preserve you from all evil: he shall preserve your soul. 8The LORD shall preserve your going out and your coming in from this time forth, and even forevermore.
