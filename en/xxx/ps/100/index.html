Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 100

1Shout joyfully unto Jehovah, all you lands! 2Serve Jehovah with gladness; come before His presence with joyful voice. 3Know that Jehovah, He is God; it is He who has made us, and not we ourselves; we are His people and the sheep of His pasture. 4Enter into His gates with thanksgiving, and into His courts with praise. Be thankful unto Him, and bless His name. 5For Jehovah is good; His mercy is eternal, and His truth endures from generation to generation.
