Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 131

1Jehovah, my heart is not haughty, nor my eyes lofty. Neither have I walked in great matters, nor in things beyond my understanding. 2Surely my soul has been calm and quiet, like a child weaned by his mother; my soul is like a weaned child. 3O Israel, hope in Jehovah from this time and forever.
