Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 149

1Praise YAH! Sing unto Jehovah a new song, and His praise in the assembly of saints. 2Let Israel rejoice in Him who made them; let the sons of Zion be joyful in their King. 3Let them praise His name in the dance; let them make music unto Him with the timbrel and harp. 4For Jehovah takes pleasure in His people; He will glorify the lowly with salvation. 5Let the saints be joyful in glory; let them shout aloud upon their beds. 6Let the exaltation of the Mighty God be in their throat, and a two-edged sword in their hand, 7to execute vengeance upon the nations, and punishment upon the peoples; 8to bind their kings with fetters, and their nobles with bonds of iron; 9to execute upon them the judgment that is written; this honor is for all His saints. Praise YAH!
