Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 124

1Unless it had been Jehovah who was for us, let Israel now say: 2Unless it had been Jehovah who was for us, when men rose up against us, 3then they would have swallowed us alive, when their wrath was kindled against us. 4Then the waters would have overwhelmed us, the stream would have gone over our soul; 5then the churning waters would have gone over our soul. 6Blessed is Jehovah, who has not given us as prey to their teeth. 7Our soul has escaped as a bird out of the snare of the fowlers; the snare is broken, and we have escaped. 8Our help is in the name of Jehovah, who made the heavens and the earth.
