Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 150

1Praise YAH! Praise the Mighty God in His sanctuary; praise Him in His mighty firmament! 2Praise Him for His mighty acts; praise Him according to His excellent greatness! 3Praise Him with the sound of the shofar; praise Him with the lute and harp! 4Praise Him with the timbrel and dance; praise Him with stringed instruments and pipes! 5Praise Him with sounding cymbals; praise Him with loud cymbals! 6Let everything that has breath praise YAH. Praise YAH!
