Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 6

1O Jehovah, rebuke me not in Your anger, nor chasten me in Your fury. 2Have mercy upon me, O Jehovah, for I am weak; O Jehovah, heal me, for my bones are anxious. 3My soul also is exceedingly anxious; but You, O Jehovah, how long? 4Return, O Jehovah, rescue my soul! Deliver me because of Your goodness! 5For in death there is no remembrance of You; in Sheol who will give thanks to You? 6I am weary from my groaning; all night I make my bed swim; I dissolve my couch with my tears. 7My eye has wasted away because of grief; it has grown weak because of all my enemies. 8Depart from me, all you workers of iniquity; for Jehovah has heard the voice of my weeping. 9Jehovah has heard my supplication; Jehovah will receive my prayer. 10Let all my enemies be ashamed and greatly dismayed; let them turn back and be ashamed in a moment.
