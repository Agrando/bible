Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 15

1O Jehovah, who may abide in Your tent? Who may dwell on Your holy hill? 2He who walks uprightly, and works righteousness, and speaks the truth in his heart; 3he who does not backbite with his tongue, nor does evil to his neighbor, nor takes up a reproach against his friend; 4in whose eyes the reprobate is despised, but he honors those who fear Jehovah; he has sworn to his hurt, and does not change it; 5he has not put out his money at interest, nor has he taken a bribe against the innocent. He who does these things shall never be moved.
