Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 87

1His foundation is in the holy mountains. 2Jehovah loves the gates of Zion more than all the dwellings of Jacob. 3Glorious things are spoken of you, O city of God! Selah 4I will make mention of Rahab and Babylon to those who know me; behold, O Philistia and Tyre, with Ethiopia: this one was born there. 5And of Zion it shall be said, This and that man have been born in her; and the Most High shall establish her. 6Jehovah will take account, when He registers the peoples: This one was born there. Selah 7Both the singers and the players on pipes say, All my springs are in you.
