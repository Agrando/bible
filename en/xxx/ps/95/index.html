Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 95

1Oh come, let us sing unto Jehovah! Let us shout joyfully unto the Rock of our salvation. 2Let us come before His presence with thanksgiving; let us shout joyfully to Him with psalms. 3For Jehovah is the great and Mighty God, and the great King above all gods. 4In His hand are the deep places of the earth; the summits of the hills are also His. 5The sea is His, for He made it; and His hands formed the dry land. 6Oh come, let us prostrate ourselves and bow down; let us kneel before Jehovah our Maker. 7For He is our God, and we are the people of His pasture, and the sheep of His hand. Today, if you will hear His voice: 8harden not your hearts, as in the strife, as in the day of testing in the wilderness, 9when your fathers tried Me; they examined Me and saw My work. 10For forty years I was grieved with that generation, and said, It is a people who go astray in their hearts, and they have not known My ways. 11So I swore in My wrath, They shall not enter into My rest.
