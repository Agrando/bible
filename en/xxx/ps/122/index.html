Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 122

1I was glad when they said unto me, let us go into the house of Jehovah. 2Our feet shall stand within your gates, O Jerusalem. 3Jerusalem is built like a city that has been joined together, 4where the tribes go up, the tribes of YAH, unto the Testimony of Israel, to give thanks unto the name of Jehovah. 5For thrones are set there for judgment, the thrones of the house of David. 6Pray for the peace of Jerusalem; may they prosper who love you. 7Peace be within your walls, quietness within your palaces. 8For the sake of my brethren and companions, I will now say, Peace be within you. 9Because of the house of Jehovah our God I will seek your good.
