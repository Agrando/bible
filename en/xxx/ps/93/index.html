Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 93

1Jehovah reigns, He is clothed with majesty; Jehovah is clothed, He has girded Himself with strength. The world also is established, so that it cannot be shaken. 2Your throne is established from of old; You are from eternity. 3The streams have lifted up, O Jehovah, the streams have lifted up their sound; the streams lift up their waves. 4Jehovah on high is mightier than the sound of many waters, than the mighty breakers of the sea. 5Your Testimonies are very sure; holiness befits Your house, O Jehovah, to the length of days.
