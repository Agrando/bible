Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 118

1O give thanks unto Jehovah, for He is good! For His mercy is eternal. 2Let Israel now say, His mercy is eternal. 3Let the house of Aaron now say, His mercy is eternal. 4Let those who fear Jehovah now say, His mercy is eternal. 5I called upon YAH in distress; YAH answered me and set me in a broad place. 6Jehovah is on my side; I will not fear. What can man do to me? 7Jehovah is for me among those who help me; therefore I shall see my desire upon those who hate me. 8It is better to trust in Jehovah than to put confidence in man. 9It is better to trust in Jehovah than to put confidence in princes. 10All the nations have surrounded me, but in the name of Jehovah I will destroy them. 11They have surrounded me, yea, they have surrounded me; but in the name of Jehovah I will destroy them. 12They have surrounded me like bees. They have been quenched like a fire of thorns, for in the name of Jehovah I will destroy them. 13You have pushed me violently, to make me fall; but Jehovah has helped me. 14YAH is my strength and song, and He has become my salvation. 15The voice of rejoicing and salvation is in the tents of the righteous; the right hand of Jehovah works with might. 16The right hand of Jehovah is exalted; the right hand of Jehovah works with might. 17I shall not die, but live, and declare the works of YAH. 18YAH has chastened and corrected me, but He has not given me over to death. 19Open unto me the gates of righteousness; I will go through them, and I will praise YAH. 20This is the gate of Jehovah, through which the righteous shall enter. 21I will praise You, for You have answered me, and have become my salvation. 22The stone which the builders have rejected has become the Head of the corner. 23This is from Jehovah; it is marvelous in our eyes. 24This is the day Jehovah has made; we will rejoice and be glad in it. 25Deliver now, I beseech You, O Jehovah; O Jehovah, I beseech You: Send now prosperity. 26Blessed is He who comes in the name of Jehovah! We have blessed you out of the house of Jehovah. 27The Mighty God is Jehovah, who shines upon us. Bind the sacrifice with cords to the horns of the altar. 28You are my Mighty God, and I will give thanks to You; You are my God, I will exalt You. 29Oh, give thanks unto Jehovah, for He is good! For His mercy is eternal.
