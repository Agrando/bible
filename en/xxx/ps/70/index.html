Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 70

1Make haste, O God, to rescue me; make haste to help me, O Jehovah. 2Let them be ashamed and abashed, those who seek after my soul; let them be turned backward and dishonored, those who desire my hurt. 3Let them be turned back as a consequence of their shame, those who say, Aha, aha! 4Let all those who seek You rejoice and be glad in You; and let those who love Your salvation say continually, Let God be magnified. 5But I am lowly and needy; make haste to me, O God! You are my help and my deliverer; O Jehovah, do not delay!
