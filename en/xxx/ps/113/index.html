Praise, O servants of Jehovah, praise the name of Jehovah! 2Blessed is the name of Jehovah from this time forth and forevermore! 3From the rising of the sun to its going down JehovahÂ’s name is to be praised. 4Jehovah is high above all nations, His glory above the heavens. 5Who is like Jehovah our God, who dwells on high, 6who humbles Himself to behold the things that are in the heavens and in the earth! 7He raises the poor out of the dust, and lifts the needy out of the ash heap, 8that He may seat him with nobles; with the nobles of His people. 9He causes the barren to dwell in a house, like a joyful mother of children.
