Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 13

1How long will You forget me, O Jehovah? Forever? How long will You hide Your face from me? 2How long shall I take counsel in my soul, having sorrow in my inner being daily? How long shall my enemy be exalted over me? 3Consider and answer me, O Jehovah my God; enlighten my eyes, lest I sleep the sleep of death; 4lest my enemy say, I have prevailed against him, and those who oppress me rejoice when I am shaken. 5But I have trusted in Your mercy; my heart shall rejoice in Your salvation. 6I will sing to Jehovah, because He has dealt bountifully with me.
