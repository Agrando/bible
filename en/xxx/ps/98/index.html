Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 98

1O sing unto Jehovah a new song! For He has done marvelous things; His right hand and His holy arm have given Him the victory. 2Jehovah has made known His salvation; His righteousness He has laid bare in the eyes of the nations. 3He has remembered His mercy and His faithfulness to the house of Israel; all the ends of the earth have seen the salvation of our God. 4Shout joyfully unto Jehovah, all the earth; break forth with praise, and make music. 5Make music unto Jehovah with the harp, with the harp and the voice of melody, 6with trumpets and the sound of a shofar; shout joyfully before Jehovah, the King. 7Let the sea roar, and all its fullness, the world and those who dwell in it; 8let the rivers clap their hands; let the hills be joyful together before Jehovah, 9for He is coming to judge the earth. With righteousness He shall judge the world, and the peoples with equity.
