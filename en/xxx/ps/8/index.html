Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 8

1O Jehovah our Lord, how excellent is Your name in all the earth! You have set Your glory above the heavens! 2Out of the mouths of babes and sucklings You have ordained strength, because of Your enemies, that You might remove the enemy and the avenger. 3When I look at Your heavens, the work of Your fingers, the moon and the stars which You have established; 4what is man that You are mindful of him, and the son of man that You visit him? 5For You have made him lacking a little from God, and have crowned him with glory and honor. 6You made him to have dominion over the works of Your hands; You have put all things under his feet: 7all sheep and oxen, even the beasts of the field; 8the birds of the heavens, and the fish of the sea passing through the paths of the seas. 9O Jehovah, our Lord, how excellent is Your name in all the earth!
