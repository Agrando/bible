Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 135

1Praise YAH! Praise the name of Jehovah; praise Him, O you servants of Jehovah! 2You who stand in the house of Jehovah, in the courts of the house of our God, 3praise YAH, for Jehovah is good; make music unto His name, for it is delightful. 4For YAH has chosen Jacob unto Himself, Israel for His valued treasure. 5For I know that Jehovah is great, and our Lord is above all gods. 6Whatever Jehovah pleases, He does in the heavens and in earth, in the seas and in all deep places. 7He causes the vapors to ascend from the ends of the earth; He makes lightning for the rain; He brings the wind out of His storehouses. 8He has struck the firstborn of Egypt, both of man and beast. 9He sent signs and wonders into your midst, O Egypt, upon Pharaoh and all his servants. 10He struck many nations and slew mighty kings; 11Sihon king of the Amorites, Og king of Bashan, and all the kingdoms of Canaan; 12and gave their land as a heritage, a heritage to Israel His people. 13Your name, O Jehovah, is eternal, Your remembrance, O Jehovah, from generation to generation. 14For Jehovah will judge His people, and He will have compassion on His servants. 15The idols of the nations are silver and gold, the work of menÂ’s hands. 16They have mouths, but they do not speak; eyes they have, but they do not see; 17they have ears, but they do not hear; nor is there any breath in their mouths. 18Those who make them are like them; everyone who trusts in them. 19Bless Jehovah, O house of Israel! Bless Jehovah, O house of Aaron! 20Bless Jehovah, O house of Levi! You who fear Jehovah, bless Jehovah! 21Blessed is Jehovah out of Zion, who dwells in Jerusalem! Praise YAH!
