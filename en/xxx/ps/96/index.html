Language > English > VW-Edition 2006 > Psalms > Chapter 96

1Oh, sing unto Jehovah a new song! Sing unto Jehovah, all the earth. 2Sing unto Jehovah, bless His name; proclaim the good news of His salvation from day to day. 3Declare His glory among the nations, His wonders among all peoples. 4For Jehovah is great and greatly to be praised; He is to be feared above all gods. 5For all the gods of the peoples are idols, but Jehovah made the heavens. 6Honor and majesty are before Him; strength and beauty are in His sanctuary. 7Ascribe unto Jehovah, O families of the peoples, ascribe unto Jehovah glory and strength. 8Ascribe unto Jehovah the glory due His name; bring an offering, and come into His courts. 9O bow down before Jehovah in the beauty of holiness! Tremble before Him, all the earth. 10Say among the nations, Jehovah reigns; the world also is firmly established, it shall not be shaken; He shall judge the peoples with equity. 11Let the heavens rejoice, and let the earth be glad; let the sea roar, and all its fullness; 12let the field be joyful, and all that is in it. Then all the trees of the forests shall rejoice before Jehovah. 13For He is coming, for He is coming to judge the earth. He shall judge the world with righteousness, and the peoples with His truth.
