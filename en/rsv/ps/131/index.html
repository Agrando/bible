Language > English > Revised Standard Version > Psalms > Chapter 131

1A Song of Ascents. Of David. 2O LORD, my heart is not lifted up, my eyes are not raised too high; I do not occupy myself with things too great and too marvelous for me. 3But I have calmed and quieted my soul, like a child quieted at its mother's breast; like a child that is quieted is my soul. [ (Psalms 131:4) O Israel, hope in the LORD from this time forth and for evermore. ]
