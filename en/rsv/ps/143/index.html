Language > English > Revised Standard Version > Psalms > Chapter 143

1A Psalm of David. 2Hear my prayer, O LORD; give ear to my supplications! In thy faithfulness answer me, in thy righteousness! 3Enter not into judgment with thy servant; for no man living is righteous before thee. 4For the enemy has pursued me; he has crushed my life to the ground; he has made me sit in darkness like those long dead. 5Therefore my spirit faints within me; my heart within me is appalled. 6I remember the days of old, I meditate on all that thou hast done; I muse on what thy hands have wrought. 7I stretch out my hands to thee; my soul thirsts for thee like a parched land. [Selah] 8Make haste to answer me, O LORD! My spirit fails! Hide not thy face from me, lest I be like those who go down to the Pit. 9Let me hear in the morning of thy steadfast love, for in thee I put my trust. Teach me the way I should go, for to thee I lift up my soul. 10Deliver me, O LORD, from my enemies! I have fled to thee for refuge! 11Teach me to do thy will, for thou art my God! Let thy good spirit lead me on a level path! 12For thy name's sake, O LORD, preserve my life! In thy righteousness bring me out of trouble! [ (Psalms 143:13) And in thy steadfast love cut off my enemies, and destroy all my adversaries, for I am thy servant. ]
