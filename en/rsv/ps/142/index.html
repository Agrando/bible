Language > English > Revised Standard Version > Psalms > Chapter 142

1A Maskil of David, when he was in the cave. A Prayer. 2I cry with my voice to the LORD, with my voice I make supplication to the LORD, 3I pour out my complaint before him, I tell my trouble before him. 4When my spirit is faint, thou knowest my way! In the path where I walk they have hidden a trap for me. 5I look to the right and watch, but there is none who takes notice of me; no refuge remains to me, no man cares for me. 6I cry to thee, O LORD; I say, Thou art my refuge, my portion in the land of the living. 7Give heed to my cry; for I am brought very low! Deliver me from my persecutors; for they are too strong for me! [ (Psalms 142:8) Bring me out of prison, that I may give thanks to thy name! The righteous will surround me; for thou wilt deal bountifully with me. ]
