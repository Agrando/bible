Language > English > Revised Standard Version > Psalms > Chapter 128

1A Song of Ascents. 2Blessed is every one who fears the LORD, who walks in his ways! 3You shall eat the fruit of the labor of your hands; you shall be happy, and it shall be well with you. 4Your wife will be like a fruitful vine within your house; your children will be like olive shoots around your table. 5Lo, thus shall the man be blessed who fears the LORD. 6The LORD bless you from Zion! May you see the prosperity of Jerusalem all the days of your life! [ (Psalms 128:7) May you see your children's children! Peace be upon Israel! ]
