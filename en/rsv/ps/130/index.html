Language > English > Revised Standard Version > Psalms > Chapter 130

1A Song of Ascents. 2Out of the depths I cry to thee, O LORD! 3Lord, hear my voice! Let thy ears be attentive to the voice of my supplications! 4If thou, O LORD, shouldst mark iniquities, Lord, who could stand? 5But there is forgiveness with thee, that thou mayest be feared. 6I wait for the LORD, my soul waits, and in his word I hope; 7my soul waits for the LORD more than watchmen for the morning, more than watchmen for the morning. 8O Israel, hope in the LORD! For with the LORD there is steadfast love, and with him is plenteous redemption. [ (Psalms 130:9) And he will redeem Israel from all his iniquities. ]
