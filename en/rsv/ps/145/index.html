1A Song of Praise. Of David. 2I will extol thee, my God and King, and bless thy name for ever and ever. 3Every day I will bless thee, and praise thy name for ever and ever. 4Great is the LORD, and greatly to be praised, and his greatness is unsearchable. 5One generation shall laud thy works to another, and shall declare thy mighty acts. 6On the glorious splendor of thy majesty, and on thy wondrous works, I will meditate. 7Men shall proclaim the might of thy terrible acts, and I will declare thy greatness. 8They shall pour forth the fame of thy abundant goodness, and shall sing aloud of thy righteousness. 9The LORD is gracious and merciful, slow to anger and abounding in steadfast love. 10The LORD is good to all, and his compassion is over all that he has made. 11All thy works shall give thanks to thee, O LORD, and all thy saints shall bless thee! 12They shall speak of the glory of thy kingdom, and tell of thy power, 13to make known to the sons of men thy mighty deeds, and the glorious splendor of thy kingdom. 14Thy kingdom is an everlasting kingdom, and thy dominion endures throughout all generations. The LORD is faithful in all his words, and gracious in all his deeds. 15The LORD upholds all who are falling, and raises up all who are bowed down. 16The eyes of all look to thee, and thou givest them their food in due season. 17Thou openest thy hand, thou satisfiest the desire of every living thing. 18The LORD is just in all his ways, and kind in all his doings. 19The LORD is near to all who call upon him, to all who call upon him in truth. 20He fulfils the desire of all who fear him, he also hears their cry, and saves them. 21The LORD preserves all who love him; but all the wicked he will destroy. [ (Psalms 145:22) My mouth will speak the praise of the LORD, and let all flesh bless his holy name for ever and ever. ]
