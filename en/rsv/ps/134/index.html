Language > English > Revised Standard Version > Psalms > Chapter 134

1A Song of Ascents. 2Come, bless the LORD, all you servants of the LORD, who stand by night in the house of the LORD! 3Lift up your hands to the holy place, and bless the LORD! [ (Psalms 134:4) May the LORD bless you from Zion, he who made heaven and earth! ]
