Language > English > Revised Standard Version > Psalms > Chapter 121

1A Song of Ascents. 2I lift up my eyes to the hills. From whence does my help come? 3My help comes from the LORD, who made heaven and earth. 4He will not let your foot be moved, he who keeps you will not slumber. 5Behold, he who keeps Israel will neither slumber nor sleep. 6The LORD is your keeper; the LORD is your shade on your right hand. 7The sun shall not smite you by day, nor the moon by night. 8The LORD will keep you from all evil; he will keep your life. [ (Psalms 121:9) The LORD will keep your going out and your coming in from this time forth and for evermore. ]
