Language > English > Revised Standard Version > Psalms > Chapter 15

1A Psalm of David. 2O LORD, who shall sojourn in thy tent? Who shall dwell on thy holy hill? 3He who walks blamelessly, and does what is right, and speaks truth from his heart; 4who does not slander with his tongue, and does no evil to his friend, nor takes up a reproach against his neighbor; 5in whose eyes a reprobate is despised, but who honors those who fear the LORD; who swears to his own hurt and does not change; [ (Psalms 15:6) who does not put out his money at interest, and does not take a bribe against the innocent. He who does these things shall never be moved. ]
