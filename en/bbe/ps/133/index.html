Language > English > Bible in Basic English > Psalms > Chapter 133

1A Song of the going up. Of David. See how good and how pleasing it is for brothers to be living together in harmony! 2It is like oil of great price on the head, flowing down over the face, even Aaron's face: coming down to the edge of his robe; 3Like the dew of Hermon, which comes down on the mountains of Zion: for there the Lord gave orders for the blessing, even life for ever.
