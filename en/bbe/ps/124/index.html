Language > English > Bible in Basic English > Psalms > Chapter 124

1A Song of the going up. Of David. If it had not been the Lord who was on our side (let Israel now say); 2If it had not been the Lord who was on our side, when men came up against us; 3They would have made a meal of us while still living, in the heat of their wrath against us: 4We would have been covered by the waters; the streams would have gone over our soul; 5Yes, the waters of pride would have gone over our soul. 6Praise be to the Lord, who has not let us be wounded by their teeth. 7Our soul has gone free like a bird out of the net of those who would take her: the net is broken, and we are free. 8Our help is in the name of the Lord, the maker of heaven and earth.
