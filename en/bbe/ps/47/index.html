1To the chief music-maker. A Psalm. Of the sons of Korah. 2\47:1\O make a glad noise with your hands, all you peoples; letting your voices go up to God with joy. 3\47:2\For the Lord Most High is to be feared; he is a great King over all the earth. 4\47:3\He will put down the peoples under us, and the nations under our feet. 5\47:4\He will give us our heritage, the glory of Jacob who is dear to him. (Selah.) 6\47:5\God has gone up with a glad cry, the Lord with the sound of the horn. 7\47:6\Give praises to God, make songs of praise; give praises to our King, make songs of praise. 8\47:7\For God is the King of all the earth; make songs of praise with knowledge. 9\47:8\God is the ruler over the nations; God is on the high seat of his holy rule. 10\47:9\The rulers of the peoples have come together, with the people of the God of Abraham; because the powers of the earth are God's: he is lifted up on high.
