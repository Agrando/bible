1A Psalm. Of David. The Lord said to my lord, Be seated at my right hand, till I put all those who are against you under your feet. 2The Lord will send out the rod of your strength from Zion; be king over your haters. 3Your people give themselves gladly in the day of your power; like the dew of the morning on the holy mountains is the army of your young men. 4The Lord has made an oath, and will not take it back. You are a priest for ever, after the order of Melchizedek. 5In the day of his wrath kings will be wounded by the Lord at your right hand. 6He will be judge among the nations, the valleys will be full of dead bodies; the head over a great country will be wounded by him. 7He will take of the stream by the way; so his head will be lifted up.
