Language > English > Bible in Basic English > Psalms > Chapter 150

1Let the Lord be praised. Give praise to God in his holy place: give him praise in the heaven of his power. 2Give him praise for his acts of power: give him praise in the measure of his great strength. 3Give him praise with the sound of the horn: give him praise with corded instruments of music. 4Give him praise with instruments of brass and in the dance: give him praise with horns and corded instruments. 5Give him praise with the loud brass: give him praise with the high-sounding brass. 6Let everything which has breath give praise to the Lord. Let the Lord be praised.
