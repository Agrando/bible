Language > English > Modern KJV 1963 > Psalms > Chapter 100

1A Psalm of praise. Make a joyful noise to the LORD, all you lands. 2Worship the LORD with gladness; come before His presence with singing. 3Know that the LORD, He [is] God. He has made us, and not we ourselves; we are His people, and the sheep of His pasture. 4Enter into His gates with thanksgiving, [and] into His courts with praise; be thankful to Him, [and] bless His name. 5For the LORD [is] good; His mercy [is] everlasting; and His truth [endures] to all generations.
