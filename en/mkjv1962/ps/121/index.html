Language > English > Modern KJV 1963 > Psalms > Chapter 121

1A Song of degrees. I will lift up my eyes to the hills. Where shall my help come from? 2My help [comes] from the LORD, who made Heaven and earth. 3He will not allow your foot to be moved; He who keeps you will not slumber. 4Behold, He who keeps Israel shall neither slumber nor sleep. 5The LORD [is] your keeper; the LORD [is] your shade on your right hand. 6The sun shall not strike you by day, nor the moon by night. 7The LORD shall keep you from all evil; He shall keep your soul. 8The LORD shall keep your going out and your coming in from this time forth, and even forevermore.
