Language > English > Modern KJV 1963 > Psalms > Chapter 29

1A Psalm of David. Give to the LORD, O you mighty, give to the LORD glory and strength. 2Give to the LORD the glory due to His name; worship the LORD in the beauty of holiness. 3The voice of the LORD [is] on the waters; the God of glory thunders; the LORD [is] above many waters. 4The voice of the LORD [is] in power, the voice of the LORD [is] in majesty. 5The voice of the LORD breaks the cedars; yea, the LORD breaks the cedars of Lebanon. 6He also makes them to skip like a calf, Lebanon and Sirion like a young wild ox. 7The voice of the LORD goes through flames of fire. 8The voice of the LORD shakes the wilderness; the LORD shakes the wilderness of Kadesh. 9The voice of the LORD causes the does to calve, and uncovers the forests; and in His temple all of it [is] saying, Glory! 10The LORD sits on the flood; yea, the LORD sits as King forever. 11The LORD will give strength to His people; the LORD will bless His people with peace.
