Language > English > Modern KJV 1963 > Psalms > Chapter 122

1A Song of degrees; of David. I was glad when they said to me, Let us go into the house of the LORD. 2Our feet shall stand within your gates, O Jerusalem. 3Jerusalem is built like a city that [is] all joined together as one; 4where the tribes go up, the tribes of the LORD, to the testimony of Israel, to give thanks to the name of the LORD. 5For there were set the thrones of judgment, the thrones of the house of David. 6Pray for the peace of Jerusalem; those who love you shall be blessed. 7Peace [be] within your walls, blessings within your palaces. 8For my brothers and companions' sakes, I will now say, Peace [be] within you. 9Because of the house of the LORD our God I will seek your good.
