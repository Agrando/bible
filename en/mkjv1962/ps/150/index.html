Language > English > Modern KJV 1963 > Psalms > Chapter 150

1Praise the LORD. Praise God in His sanctuary; praise Him in the expanse of His power. 2Praise Him for His mighty acts; praise Him according to His excellent greatness. 3Praise Him with the sound of the trumpet; praise Him with the harp and lyre. 4Praise Him with the timbrel and dance; praise Him with stringed instruments and pipes. 5Praise Him on the sounding cymbals; praise Him with the resounding cymbals. 6Let everything that breathes praise the LORD. Praise the LORD!
