Language > English > Modern KJV 1963 > Psalms > Chapter 128

1A Song of degrees. Blessed [is] everyone who fears the LORD, who walks in His ways. 2For you shall surely eat the labor of your hands; you [shall be] happy, and all [is] well with you. 3Your wife [shall be] like a fruitful vine by the sides of your house; your sons [shall be] like olive plants around your table. 4Behold! So shall the man be blessed who fears the LORD. 5The LORD shall bless you out of Zion; and you shall see the good of Jerusalem all the days of your life. 6Yea, you shall see the sons of your sons. Peace [be] on Israel.
