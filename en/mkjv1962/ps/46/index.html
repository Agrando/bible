Language > English > Modern KJV 1963 > Psalms > Chapter 46

1To the Chief Musician. For the sons of Korah. A Song For the Virgins. God [is] our refuge and strength, a very present help in trouble. 2Therefore we will not fear when the earth changes, and when mountains [are] slipping into the heart of the seas. 3Let its waters roar and foam; let the mountains shake with the swelling of it. Selah. 4[There is] a river, its channels gladden the city of God glad, the holy [place] of the tabernacles of the Most High. 5God [is] in the midst of her; she shall not be moved; God shall help her at the turning of the morning. 6The nations raged, the kingdoms were shaken; He uttered His voice, the earth melted. 7The LORD of hosts [is] with us; the God of Jacob [is] our refuge. Selah. 8Come, behold the works of the LORD, who makes ruins on the earth; 9who makes wars to cease to the ends of the earth; He breaks the bow, and cuts the spear in two; He burns the chariots in the fire. 10Be still, and know that I [am] God! I will be praised among the nations, I will be praised in the earth. 11The LORD of hosts [is] with us; the God of Jacob [is] our refuge. Selah.
