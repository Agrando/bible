Language > English > Modern KJV 1963 > Psalms > Chapter 123

1A Song of degrees. To You I lift up my eyes, O Dweller in Heaven. 2Behold, as the eyes of servants [look] to the hand of their masters; as the eyes of a maiden to the hand of her mistress; so our eyes wait on the LORD our God, until He shall have mercy on us. 3Have mercy on us, O LORD, have mercy on us; for we are exceedingly filled with scorn. 4Our soul is exceedingly filled [with] the contempt of those who are at ease, with the scorn of the proud.
