Language > English > Modern KJV 1963 > Psalms > Chapter 93

1The LORD reigns, He is clothed with majesty; the LORD is clothed with strength. He clothed Himself and the world is established; it shall not be shaken. 2Your throne [is] established of old; You [are] from everlasting. 3The floods have lifted up, O LORD, the floods have lifted up their voices; the floods lift up their waves. 4The LORD on high [is] mightier than the noise of many waters, than the mighty waves of the sea. 5Your testimonies are very sure; holiness becomes Your house forever, O LORD.
