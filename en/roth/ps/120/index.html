Language > English > Rotherham Version > Psalms > Chapter 120

1Unto Yahweh, in the distress that befell me, I criedand he answered me. 2O Yahweh! rescue thou my soulfrom the false lip, from the deceitful tongue. 3What shall be given to thee, and what shall be added to thee, thou deceitful tongue? 4The arrows of the hero sharpened, with burning coals of broom. 5Woe is me, That I sojourn in Meshek,That I abide near the tents of Kedar! 6Long, hath my soul had her dwelling with him that hateth peace: 7I, am for peace, and verily I speak, They, are for war!
