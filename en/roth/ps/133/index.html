Language > English > Rotherham Version > Psalms > Chapter 133

1Lo! how good and how delightful, for brethren, to dwell together even as one. 2Like the precious oil upon the head, descending upon the beard; the beard of Aaron,which descended unto the opening of his robe: 3Like the dew of Hermon, which descended upon the mountains of Zion,for, there, did Yahweh command the blessing, Life, unto times age-abiding?
