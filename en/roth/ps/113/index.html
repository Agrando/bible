Language > English > Rotherham Version > Psalms > Chapter 113

1Praise ye Yah! Praise, O ye servants of Yahweh, Praise the Name of Yahweh; 2Be the Name of Yahweh blessed, henceforth, even to times age-abiding. 3From the rising of the sun unto the going in thereof, Worthy to be praised, is the Name of Yahweh: 4High above all nations, is Yahweh, Above the heavens, his glory. 5Who is like Yahweh our God? that goeth on high to dwell, 6That cometh down low to look, through the heavens and through the earth; 7That raiseth, out of the dust, the poor, From the dunghill, uplifteth the needy; 8To give a seat with nobles, with the nobles of his people! 9Causing the barren woman to dwell in household, A mother of sons in her joy! Praise ye Yah.
