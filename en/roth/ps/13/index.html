Language > English > Rotherham Version > Psalms > Chapter 13

1How long, O Yahweh, wilt thou wholly forget me? How long wilt thou hide thy face from me? 2How long shall I lay up cares within my soul, sorrow in my heart, day by day? How long shall mine enemy lift himself up over me? 3Have regard! answer me O Yahweh my God,Light up mine eyes, lest I sleep on into death: 4Lest mine enemy say, I have prevailed over him! and, mine adversaries, exult, that I totter. 5But, I, in thy lovingkindness, have put my trust, My heart shall exult in thy salvation: 6I will sing to Yahweh, for he hath dealt beautifully with me.
