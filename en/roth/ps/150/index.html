Language > English > Rotherham Version > Psalms > Chapter 150

1Praise ye Yah, Praise ye GOD in his sanctuary, Praise him, in his strong expanse: 2Praise him, for his mighty deeds, Praise him, according to his exceeding greatness: 3Praise him, with the blast of a horn, Praise him, with the harp and lyre: 4Praise him, with timbrel and dance,Praise him, with stringed instrument and flute, 5Praise him, with cymbals of clear tone,Praise him, with cymbals of loud clang: 6Let, every breathing thing, praise Yah, Praise ye Yah!
