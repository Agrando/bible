Language > English > John Wycliffe Bible > Psalms > Chapter 149

1The `title of the hundrid and nyne and fourtithe salm. Alleluya. Synge ye to the Lord a newe song; hise heriyng be in the chirche of seyntis. 2Israel be glad in hym that made hym; and the douytris of Syon make ful out ioye in her king. 3Herie thei his name in a queer; seie thei salm to hym in a tympan, and sautre. 4For the Lord is wel plesid in his puple; and he hath reisid mylde men in to heelthe. 5Seyntis schulen make ful out ioye in glorie; thei schulen be glad in her beddis. 6The ful out ioiyngis of God in the throte of hem; and swerdis scharp on `ech side in the hondis of hem. 7To do veniaunce in naciouns; blamyngis in puplis. 8To bynde the kyngis of hem in stockis; and the noble men of hem in yrun manaclis. 9That thei make in hem doom writun; this is glorye to alle hise seyntis.
