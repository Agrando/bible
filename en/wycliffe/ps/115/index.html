Language > English > John Wycliffe Bible > Psalms > Chapter 115

1I bileuede, for which thing Y spak; forsoth Y was maad low ful myche. 2I seide in my passing; Ech man is a lier. 3What schal Y yelde to the Lord; for alle thingis which he yeldide to me? 4I schal take the cuppe of heelthe; and Y schal inwardli clepe the name of the Lord. 5I schal yelde my vowis to the Lord bifor al his puple; 6the deth of seyntis of the Lord is precious in his siyt. 7O! Lord, for Y am thi seruant; Y am thi seruaunt, and the sone of thi handmaide. Thou hast broke my bondys, 8to thee Y schal offre a sacrifice of heriyng; and Y schal inwardli clepe the name of the Lord. 9I schal yelde my vowis to the Lord, in the siyt of al his puple; 10in the porchis of the hous of the Lord, in the myddil of thee, Jerusalem.
