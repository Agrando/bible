Language > English > John Wycliffe Bible > Psalms > Chapter 120

1The `title of the hundrid and twentithe salm. The song of greces. I reiside myn iyen to the hillis; fro whannus help schal come to me. 2Myn help is of the Lord; that made heuene and erthe. 3The Lord yyue not thi foot in to mouyng; nether he nappe, that kepith thee. 4Lo! he schal not nappe, nether slepe; that kepith Israel. 5The Lord kepith thee; the Lord is thi proteccioun aboue thi riythond. 6The sunne schal not brenne thee bi dai; nether the moone bi nyyt. 7The Lord kepe thee fro al yuel; the Lord kepe thi soule. 8The Lord kepe thi goyng in and thi goyng out; fro this tyme now and in to the world.
