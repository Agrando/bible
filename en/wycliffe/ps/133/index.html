Language > English > John Wycliffe Bible > Psalms > Chapter 133

1The `title of the hundrid and thre and thrittithe salm. The song of greces. Lo! now blesse ye the Lord; alle the seruauntis of the Lord. Ye that stonden in the hous of the Lord; in the hallis of `the hous of oure God. 2In nyytis reise youre hondis in to hooli thingis; and blesse ye the Lord. 3The Lord blesse thee fro Syon; which Lord made heuene and erthe.
