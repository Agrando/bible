Language > English > John Wycliffe Bible > Psalms > Chapter 127

1The title of the hundrid and seuene and twentithe salm. The song of greces. Blessid ben alle men, that dreden the Lord; that gon in hise weies. 2For thou schalt ete the trauels of thin hondis; thou art blessid, and it schal be wel to thee. 3Thi wijf as a plenteous vyne; in the sidis of thin hous. Thi sones as the newe sprenges of olyue trees; in the cumpas of thi bord. 4Lo! so a man schal be blessid; that dredith the Lord. 5The Lord blesse thee fro Syon; and se thou the goodis of Jerusalem in alle the daies of thi lijf. 6And se thou the sones of thi sones; se thou pees on Israel.
