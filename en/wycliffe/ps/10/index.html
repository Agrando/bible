Language > English > John Wycliffe Bible > Psalms > Chapter 10

1The title of the tenthe salm. To the victorie of Dauid. 2I triste in the Lord; hou seien ye to my soule, Passe thou ouere in to an hil, as a sparowe doith? 3For lo! synneris han bent a bouwe; thei han maad redi her arowis in an arowe caas; `for to schete in derknesse riytful men in herte. 4For thei han distryed, whom thou hast maad perfit; but what dide the riytful man? 5The Lord is in his hooli temple; he is Lord, his seete is in heuene. Hise iyen biholden on a pore man; hise iyelidis axen the sones of men. 6The Lord axith a iust man, and vnfeithful man; but he, that loueth wickidnesse, hatith his soule. 7He schal reyne snaris on `synful men; fier, brymston, and the spirit of tempestis ben the part of the cuppe of hem. 8For the Lord is riytful, and louede riytfulnessis; his cheer siy equite, `ethir euennesse.
