Language > English > John Wycliffe Bible > Psalms > Chapter 130

1The title of the hundrid and thrittithe salm. The song of greces, `to Dauith himself. Lord, myn herte is not enhaunsid; nether myn iyen ben reisid. Nether Y yede in the grete thingis; nether in merueilis aboue me. 2If Y feelide not mekely; but enhaunside my soule. As a childe wenyde on his modir; so yelding be in my soule. 3Israel hope in the Lord; fro this tyme now and in to the world.
