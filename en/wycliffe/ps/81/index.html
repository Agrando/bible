Language > English > John Wycliffe Bible > Psalms > Chapter 81

1The title of the oon and eiytetithe salm. Of Asaph. God stood in the synagoge of goddis; forsothe he demeth goddis in the myddil. 2Hou longe demen ye wickidnesse; and taken the faces of synneris? 3Deme ye to the nedi man, and to the modirles child; iustifie ye the meke man and pore. 4Raueische ye out a pore man; and delyuere ye the nedi man fro the hond of the synner. 5Thei knewen not, nether vndirstoden, thei goen in derknessis; alle the foundementis of erthe schulen be moued. 6I seide, Ye ben goddis; and alle ye ben the sones of hiy God. 7But ye schulen die as men; and ye schulen falle doun as oon of the princis. 8Ryse, thou God, deme thou the erthe; for thou schalt haue eritage in alle folkis.
