Language > English > John Wycliffe Bible > Psalms > Chapter 46

1The title of the sixte and fourtithe salm. To victorie, a salm to the sones of Chore. 2Alle ye folkis, make ioie with hondis; synge ye hertli to God in the vois of ful out ioiyng. 3For the Lord is hiy and ferdful; a greet kyng on al erthe. 4He made puplis suget to vs; and hethene men vndur oure feet. 5He chees his eritage to vs; the fairnesse of Jacob, whom he louyde. 6God stiede in hertli song; and the Lord in the vois of a trumpe. 7Synge ye to oure God, synge ye; synge ye to oure kyng, synge ye. 8For God is kyng of al erthe; synge ye wiseli. 9God schal regne on hethene men; God sittith on his hooli seete. 10The princes of puplis ben gaderid togidere with God of Abraham; for the stronge goddis of erthe ben reisid greetli.
