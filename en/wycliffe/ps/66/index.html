Language > English > John Wycliffe Bible > Psalms > Chapter 66

1The titil of the sixe and sixtithe salm. `In Ebreu thus, To the victorie in orguns, the salm of the song. `In Jerom `thus, To the ouercomer in salmes, the song of writing of a delitable thing with metre. 2God haue merci on vs, and blesse vs; liytne he his cheer on vs, and haue merci on vs. 3That we knowe thi weie on erthe; thin heelthe in alle folkis. 4God, puplis knowleche to thee; alle puplis knouleche to thee. 5Hethen men be glad, and make fulli ioye, for thou demest puplis in equite; and dressist hethene men in erthe. 6God, puplis knouleche to thee, alle puplis knouleche to thee; 7the erthe yaf his fruyt. God, oure God blesse vs, God blesse vs; and alle the coostis of erthe drede hym.
