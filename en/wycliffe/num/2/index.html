Language > English > John Wycliffe Bible > Numbers > Chapter 2

1And the Lord spak to Moises and to Aaron, and seide, Alle men of the sones of Israel schulen sette tentis bi the cumpenyes, signes, and baneris, and housis of her kynredis, bi the cumpas of the tabernacle of boond of pees. 2At the est Judas schal sette tentis, bi the cumpenyes of his oost; and Naason, the sone of Amynadab, schal be prince of the sones of Juda; 3and al the summe of fiyteris of his kynrede, foure and seuenty thousynde and sixe hundrid. 4Men of the lynage of Ysachar settiden tentis bysydis hym, of whiche the prince was Nathanael, the sone of Suar; 5and al the noumbre of hise fiyteris, foure and fifti thousynde and foure hundrid. 6Eliab, the sone of Elon, was prince of the lynage of Zabulon; 7al the oost of fiyteris of his kynrede, seuene and fifti thousynde and foure hundrid. 8Alle that weren noumbrid in the castels of Judas, weren an hundrid thousynde `foure scoore thousynde and sixe and foure hundrid; and thei schulen go out the firste bi her cumpanyes. 9In the castels of the sones of Ruben, at the south coost, Elisur, the sone of Sedeur, schal be prince; and al the oost of hise fiyteris, 10that weren noumbrid, sixe and fourti thousynde and fyue hundrid. 11Men of the lynage of Symeon settiden tentis bisidis hym, of whiche the prince was Salamyhel, the sone of Surisaddai; and al the oost of hise fiyteris, 12that weren noumbrid, nyne and fifty thousynde and thre hundrid. 13Eliasaph, sone of Duel, was prince in the lynage of Gad; and al the oost of his fiyteris, 14that weren noumbrid, fyue and fourti thousynde sixe hundrid and fifti. 15Alle that weren noumbrid in the castels of Ruben, an hundrid thousynde fifty thousinde and a thousinde foure hundrid and fifty; thei schulen go forth in the secounde place bi her cumpenyes. 16Sotheli the tabernacle of witnessyng schal be reisid bi the offices of dekenes, and bi the cumpenyes `of hem; as it schal be reisid, so and it schal be takun doun; alle schulen go forth bi her places and ordris. 17The castels of the sones of Effraym schulen be at the west coost, of whiche the prince was Elisama, the sone of Amyud; 18and al the oost of his fiyteris, that weren noumbrid, fourti thousynde and fyue hundrid. 19And with hem was the lynage of `the sones of Manasses, of whiche the prince was Gamaliel, the sone of Fadassur; 20al the oost of hise fiyteris, that weren noumbrid, two and thretti thousande and two hundrid. 21In the lynage of the sones of Beniamyn the prince was Abidan, the sone of Gedeon; 22and al the oost of hise fiyteris, that weren noumbrid, fyue and thretti thousynde and foure hundrid. 23Alle that weren noumbrid in the castels of Effraym weren an hundrid thousynde and eiyte thousynde and oon hundrid; thei schulen go forth `the thridde bi her cumpenyes. 24At the `part of the north the sones of Dan settiden tentis, of whiche the prince was Abiezer, the sone of Amysaddai; 25al the oost of hise fiyteris, that weren noumbrid, two and sixti thousynde and seuene hundrid. 26Men of the lynage of Aser settiden tentis bisidis hym, of whiche the prince was Fegiel, the sone of Ochran; 27and al the oost of hise fiyteris, that weren noumbrid, fourti thousynde `and a thousynde and fyue hundrid. 28Of the lynage of the sones of Neptalym the prince was Ahira, the sone of Henam; and al the oost of hise fiyteris, 29thre and fifti thousynde and foure hundrid. 30Alle that weren noumbrid in the castels of Dan weren an hundrid thousynde seuene and fifti thousynde and sixe hundrid; thei schulen go forth the laste. 31This is the noumbre of the sones of Israel, bi the housis of her kynredis, and bi cumpenyes of the oost departid, sixe hundrid thousynde thre thousynde fyue hundrid and fifti. 32Sotheli the dekenes weren not noumbrid among the sones of Israel; for God comaundide so to Moises. 33And the sones of Israel diden bi alle thingis whiche the Lord comaundide; thei settiden tentis bi her cumpenyes, and yeden forth bi the meynees, and housis of her fadris.
