Language > English > World English Bible > Psalms > Chapter 15

1Yahweh, who shall dwell in your sanctuary? Who shall live on your holy hill? 2He who walks blamelessly, does what is right, And speaks truth in his heart; 3He who doesn`t slander with his tongue, Nor does evil to his friend, Nor casts slurs against his fellow man; 4In whose eyes a vile man is despised, But who honors those who fear Yahweh; He who keeps an oath even when it hurts, and doesn`t change; 5He who doesn`t lend out his money for usury, Nor take a bribe against the innocent. He who does these things shall never be shaken. Psalm 16 A Poem by David.
