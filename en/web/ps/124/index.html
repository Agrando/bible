Language > English > World English Bible > Psalms > Chapter 124

1If it had not been Yahweh who was on our side, Let Israel now say, 2If it had not been Yahweh who was on our side, When men rose up against us; 3Then they would have swallowed us up alive, When their wrath was kindled against us; 4Then the waters would have overwhelmed us, The stream would have gone over our soul; 5Then the proud waters would have gone over our soul. 6Blessed be Yahweh, Who has not given us as a prey to their teeth. 7Our soul has escaped like a bird out of the fowler`s snare. The snare is broken, and we have escaped. 8Our help is in the name of Yahweh, Who made heaven and earth. Psalm 125 A Song of Ascents.
