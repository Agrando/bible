Language > English > New Heart English Bible > Psalms > Chapter 145

1A praise psalm by David.ALEPHI will exalt you, my God, the King.I will praise your name forever and ever.BET 2Every day I will praise you.I will extol your name forever and ever.GIMEL 3Great is the Lord, and greatly to be praised!His greatness is unsearchable.DALET 4One generation will commend your works to another,and will declare your mighty acts.HEY 5Of the glorious majesty of your honor,of your wondrous works, I will meditate.VAV 6Men will speak of the might of your awesome acts.I will declare your greatness.ZAYIN 7They will utter the memory of your great goodness,and will sing of your righteousness.HET 8The Lord is gracious, merciful,slow to anger, and of great loving kindness.TET 9The Lord is good to all.His tender mercies are over all his works.YOD 10All your works will give thanks to you, Lord.Your holy ones will extol you.KAPH 11They will speak of the glory of your kingdom,and talk about your power;LAMED 12to make known to the sons of men his mighty acts,the glory of the majesty of his kingdom.MEM 13Your kingdom is an everlasting kingdom.Your dominion endures throughout all generations.NUNthe Lord is faithful in all his words,and loving in all his deeds.SAMEKH 14The Lord upholds all who fall,and raises up all those who are bowed down.AYIN 15The eyes of all wait for you.You give them their food in due season.PEY 16You open your hand,and satisfy the desire of every living thing.TZADE 17The Lord is righteous in all his ways,and gracious in all his works.QOPH 18The Lord is near to all those who call on him,to all who call on him in truth.RESH 19He will fulfill the desire of those who fear him.He also will hear their cry, and will save them.SHIN 20The Lord preserves all those who love him,but all the wicked he will destroy.TAV 21My mouth will speak the praise of the Lord.Let all flesh bless his holy name forever and ever.
