1A Song of Ascents. By David.See how good and how pleasant it isfor brothers to live together in unity! 2It is like the precious oil on the head,that ran down on the beard,even Aaron's beard;that came down on the edge of his robes; 3like the dew of Hermon,that comes down on the hills of Zion:for there the Lord gives the blessing,even life forevermore.
