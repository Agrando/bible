Language > English > English Jubilee 2000 Bible > Job > Chapter 25

1Then Bildad, the Shuhite, answered, and said, 2Dominion and fear [are] with [God]; he makes peace in his high places. 3Is there any number to his armies? And upon whom does his light not arise? 4How then can man be justified with God? Or how can he that is born of a woman be clean? 5Behold, even the moon shall not shine, neither are the stars pure in his sight. 6How much less man, [who is as] a worm, and the son of man, [who is also] a worm?:
