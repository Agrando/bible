1<> Save me, O God, in thy name and defend me by thy valour. 2Hear my prayer, O God; give ear to the words of my mouth. 3For strangers are risen up against me, and oppressors seek after my soul; they have not set God before them. Selah. 4Behold, God [is] my helper; the Lord [is] with those that uphold my soul. 5He shall reward evil unto my enemies; cut them off in thy truth. 6I will freely sacrifice unto thee; I will praise thy name, O LORD; for [it is] good. 7For he has delivered me out of all trouble, and my eye has seen [his desire] upon my enemies.:
