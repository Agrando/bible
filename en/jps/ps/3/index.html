Language > English > Jewish Publication Society AT > Psalms > Chapter 3

1LORD, how many are mine adversaries become! Many are they that rise up against me. 2Many there are that say of my soul: 'There is no salvation for him in God.' Selah 3But thou, O LORD, art a shield about me; my glory, and the lifter up of my head. 4With my voice I call unto the LORD, and He answereth me out of His holy mountain. Selah 5I lay me down, and I sleep; I awake, for the LORD sustaineth me. 6I am not afraid of ten thousands of people, that have set themselves against me round about. 7Arise, O LORD; save me, O my God; for Thou hast smitten all mine enemies upon the cheek, Thou hast broken the teeth of the wicked. 8Salvation belongeth unto the LORD; Thy blessing be upon Thy people. Selah
