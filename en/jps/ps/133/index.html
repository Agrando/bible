Language > English > Jewish Publication Society AT > Psalms > Chapter 133

1A Song of Ascents; of David. Behold, how good and how pleasant it is for brethren to dwell together in unity! 2It is like the precious oil upon the head, coming down upon the beard; even Aaron's beard, that cometh down upon the collar of his garments; 3Like the dew of Hermon, that cometh down upon the mountains of Zion; for there the LORD commanded the blessing, even life for ever.
