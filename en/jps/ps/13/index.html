Language > English > Jewish Publication Society AT > Psalms > Chapter 13

1How long, O LORD, wilt Thou forget me for ever? How long wilt Thou hide Thy face from me? 2How long shall I take counsel in my soul, having sorrow in my heart by day? How long shall mine enemy be exalted over me? 3Behold Thou, and answer me, O LORD my God; lighten mine eyes, lest I sleep the sleep of death; 4Lest mine enemy say: 'I have prevailed against him'; lest mine adversaries rejoice when I am moved. 5But as for me, in Thy mercy do I trust; my heart shall rejoice in Thy salvation. 6I will sing unto the LORD, because He hath dealt bountifully with me.
