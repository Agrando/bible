Language > English > Jewish Publication Society AT > Psalms > Chapter 150

1Hallelujah. Praise God in His sanctuary; praise Him in the firmament of His power. 2Praise Him for His mighty acts; praise Him according to His abundant greatness. 3Praise Him with the blast of the horn; praise Him with the psaltery and harp. 4Praise Him with the timbrel and dance; praise Him with stringed instruments and the pipe. 5Praise Him with the loud-sounding cymbals; praise Him with the clanging cymbals. 6Let every thing that hath breath praise the LORD. Hallelujah.
