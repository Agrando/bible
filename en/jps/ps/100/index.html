Language > English > Jewish Publication Society AT > Psalms > Chapter 100

1A Psalm of thanksgiving. Shout unto the LORD, all the earth. 2Serve the LORD with gladness; come before His presence with singing. 3Know ye that the LORD He is God; it is He that hath made us, and we our His, His people, and the flock of His pasture. 4Enter into His gates with thanksgiving, and into His courts with praise; give thanks unto Him, and bless His name. 5For the LORD is good; His mercy endureth for ever; and His faithfulness unto all generations.
