1+ 2\70:1\To the chief Musician, A Psalm of David to bring to remembrance. Make haste, O God, to deliver me; make haste to help me, O LORD. 3\70:2\Let them be ashamed and confounded that seek after my soul: let them be turned backward, and put to confusion, that desire my hurt. 4\70:3\Let them be turned back for a reward of their shame that say, Aha, aha. 5\70:4\Let all those that seek thee rejoice and be glad in thee: and let such as love thy salvation say continually, Let God be magnified. 6\70:5\But I am poor and needy: make haste to me, O God: thou art my help and my deliverer; O LORD, make no delay.
