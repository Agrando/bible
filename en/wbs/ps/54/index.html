1+ 2+ 3\54:1\To the chief Musician on Neginoth, Maschil, A Psalm of David, when the Ziphims came and said to Saul, Doth not David hide himself with us? Save me, O God, by thy name, and judge me by thy strength. 4\54:2\Hear my prayer, O God; give ear to the words of my mouth. 5\54:3\For strangers have risen up against me, and oppressors seek after my soul: they have not set God before them. Selah. 6\54:4\Behold, God is my helper: the Lord is with them that uphold my soul. 7\54:5\He will reward evil to my enemies; cut them off in thy truth. 8\54:6\I will freely sacrifice to thee: I will praise thy name, O LORD; for it is good. 9\54:7\For he hath delivered me out of all trouble: and my eye hath seen its desire upon my enemies.
