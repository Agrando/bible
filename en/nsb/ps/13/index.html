Language > English > New Simplified Bible > Psalms > Chapter 13

1How long, O Jehovah? Will you forget me forever? How long will you hide your face from me? 2How long must I make decisions alone with sorrow in my heart day after day? How long will my enemy triumph over me? 3Look at me! Answer me, O Jehovah my God! Light up my eyes, or else I will die. 4My enemy will say: »I have overpowered him.« My opponents will rejoice because I have been shaken. 5I trust your loving-kindness. My heart finds joy in your salvation. 6I will sing to Jehovah for he has been good to me.
