Language > English > New Simplified Bible > Psalms > Chapter 54

1Save me, O God, by your name, and judge me by your strength. 2Hear my prayer, O God; give ear to the words of my mouth. 3For strangers have risen up against me. And oppressors seek after my life. They have not kept God in their presence. 4Behold, God is my helper: Jehovah is with those who preserve my life. 5He will repay my enemies for their evil: consume destroy them in your truth. 6I will freely sacrifice to you. O Jehovah, I will praise your name for it is good. 7For he has delivered me out of all trouble: and my eye has seen his defeat of my enemies.
