Language > English > New Simplified Bible > Psalms > Chapter 29

1Give to Jehovah, you sons of the mighty ones. Give to Jehovah glory and power. 2Give to Jehovah the glory his name deserves. Worship Jehovah in his holy splendor. 3The voice of Jehovah is upon the waters. The God of glory thunders, Jehovah is over many waters. 4The voice of Jehovah is powerful. The voice of Jehovah is glorious. 5The voice of Jehovah breaks the cedars. Jehovah splinters the cedars of Lebanon. 6He makes Lebanon skip along like a calf and Mount Sirion like a wild bull. 7The voice of Jehovah cuts with flames of fire from lightning. 8The voice of Jehovah shakes the wilderness. Jehovah makes the wilderness of Kadesh tremble. 9The voice of Jehovah makes the deer to calve and strips the forests bare. In His temple everything says: »Glory!« 10Jehovah sat enthroned over the flood. Jehovah sits enthroned as king forever. 11Jehovah will give power strength to his people. Jehovah will bless his people with peace prosperityhealthsafety.
