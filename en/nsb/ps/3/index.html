Language > English > New Simplified Bible > Psalms > Chapter 3

1O Jehovah, look how my enemies have increased! Many are attacking me. 2Many are saying about me: »Even with God on his side, he will not have victory deliverancesalvation.« 3You, O Jehovah, are a protective shield over me. You are my glory. You hold my head high. 4I call aloud to Jehovah. He answers me from his holy mountain. 5I lie down and sleep. I wake up again because Jehovah continues to sustain me. 6I am not afraid of the tens of thousands who have taken positions against me on all sides. 7Arise, O Jehovah! Save me, O my God! You have slapped all my enemies in the face. You have broken the teeth of wicked people. 8Salvation belongs to Jehovah! You bless your people.
