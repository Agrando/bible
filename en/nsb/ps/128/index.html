Language > English > New Simplified Bible > Psalms > Chapter 128

1Blessed are all who reverence Jehovah and live his way. 2You will certainly eat what your own hands have provided. Blessings to you! May things go well for you! 3Your wife will be like a fruitful vine inside your home. Your children will be like young olive trees around your table. 4This is how Jehovah will bless the person who fears him. 5May Jehovah bless you from Zion so that you may see Jerusalem prosper all the days of your life. 6May you live to see your children's children. Let there be peace in Israel!
