Language > English > New Simplified Bible > Psalms > Chapter 61

1Hear my cry, O God; listen to my prayer! 2In despair and far from home I call to you! Take me to the rock that is my safe refuge, 3for you are my protector, my strong tower defense against my enemies. 4Let me live in your sanctuary all my life. Let me find safety under your wings. 5You have heard my vows, O God, and you have given me what belongs to those who respect your name. 6Add many years to the king's life; let him live on and on! 7May he rule forever in your presence, O God. Protect him with your constant love and faithfulness. 8So I will always sing praises to your name, as I perform my vows day after day.
