Language > English > New Simplified Bible > Psalms > Chapter 100

1Make a joyful noise soundshout to Jehovah, all you lands! 2Serve Jehovah with gladness and come before his presence with a joyful song. 3Know that Jehovah is God. He has made us, and not we ourselves. We are his people, and the sheep of his pasture. 4Enter into his gates with thanksgiving, into his courtyard with praise: thank him and bless his name. 5For Jehovah is good. His loving kindness is everlasting and his truth endures to all generations.
