Language > English > New Simplified Bible > 1 John > Chapter 1

1We have heard that which was from the beginning. We have also seen it with our own eyes. We looked at it and touched it with our hands. It is the Word of life. 2The life was made known and we have seen and testify about it. We declare the life to you. It is the eternal life, which was with the Father and was shown to us. 3We declare to you what we have seen and heard. This way you may have fellowship with us. Yes, and our fellowship is with the Father, and with his Son Jesus Christ! 4We write these things that our joy may be made full complete. 5This is the message we heard from him and announce to you, that God is light, and in him is no darkness at all. 6If we say we have fellowship with him and walk in the darkness, we lie, and do not live practice the truth. 7If we walk in the light, as he is in the light, we have fellowship with one another, and the blood of Jesus his Son cleanses us from all sin. 8If we say that we have no sin, we deceive ourselves, and the truth is not in us. 9If we confess our sins, he is faithful and righteous to forgive us our sins, and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness. 10If we say we have not sinned we make him a liar, and his word is not in us.
