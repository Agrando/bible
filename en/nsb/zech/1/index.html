1The word of Jehovah came to Zechariah the son of Berechiah, the son of Iddo, the prophet. It was in the eighth month, in the second year of Darius the king: 2Jehovah was very angry with your fathers. 3Tell them, Jehovah of Hosts has said: »Jehovah of Hosts says return to me and I will return to you.« 4»Do not be like your fathers!
