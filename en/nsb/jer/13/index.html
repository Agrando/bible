1Jehovah told me: »Go and buy a linen belt. Wear it on your hips for a while, but do not put it in any water.« 2So I bought a belt and put it on my hips. 3Then Jehovah said: 4»Take off the linen belt. Go to the Euphrates River and hide the belt in a crack between some large rocks.« 5And that is what I did, just as Jehovah commanded. 6Some time later Jehovah said: »Go back and get the linen belt.« 7I went back and dug the belt out of the hiding place, but the cloth had rotted, and the linen belt was ruined. It was good for nothing. 8Then Jehovah said: 9»Jeremiah, I will use Babylon to destroy the pride of the people of Judah and Jerusalem. 10»The people of Judah are evil and stubborn.
