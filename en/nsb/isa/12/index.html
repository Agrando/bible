Language > English > New Simplified Bible > Isaiah > Chapter 12

1In that day you will say: »I will praise you, O Jehovah. Although you were angry with me, your anger has turned away and you have comforted me. 2»Truly God is my salvation! I will trust and not be afraid. Jehovah Jehovah is my strength and my song! He has become my salvation.« 3With joy gladness you will draw water from the wells of salvation. 4In that day you will say: »Give thanks to Jehovah, call on his name; make known among the nations what he has done, and proclaim that his name is exalted. 5»Sing to Jehovah, for he has done glorious things. Let this be known to the entire world. 6»Shout aloud and sing for joy, people of Zion, for great is the Holy One of Israel among you.«
