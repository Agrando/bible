1Now late on the Sabbath day, as it began to dawn toward the first [day] of the week, Mary Magdalene and the other Mary came to see the tomb. 2And look, an angel appeared. His appearance was as lightning, and his clothing white as snow. 3And the angel said to the women, Don't be{+} afraid; for I know that you{+} seek Jesus, who has been crucified. 4He is not here; for he is risen, even as he said. Come, see the place where he was lying. 5And they departed quickly from the tomb with fear, and fled.
