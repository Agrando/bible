Language > English > Updated Bible Version > Psalms > Chapter 133

1A Song of Ascents; of David. Look, how good and how pleasant it is For brothers to dwell together in unity! 2It is like the precious oil on the head, That ran down on the beard, Even Aaron's beard; That came down on the skirt of his garments; 3Like the dew of Hermon, That comes down on the mountains of Zion: For there Yahweh commanded the blessing, Even life forevermore.
