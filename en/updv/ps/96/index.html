Language > English > Updated Bible Version > Psalms > Chapter 96

1Oh sing to Yahweh a new song: Sing to Yahweh, all the earth. 2Sing to Yahweh, bless his name; Proclaim the good news of his salvation from day to day. 3Declare his glory among the nations, His marvelous works among all the peoples. 4For great is Yahweh, and greatly to be praised: He is to be awed above all gods. 5For all the gods of the peoples are idols; But Yahweh made the heavens. 6Honor and majesty are before him: Strength and beauty are in his sanctuary. 7Ascribe to Yahweh, you{+} kindreds of the peoples, Ascribe to Yahweh glory and strength. 8Ascribe to Yahweh the glory due to his name: Bring an offering, and come into his courts. 9Oh worship Yahweh in holy array: Tremble before him, all the earth. 10Say among the nations, Yahweh reigns: Indeed, the world will be established, it will not be moved: He will judge the peoples with equity. 11Let the heavens be glad, and let the earth rejoice; Let the sea roar, and the fullness of it; 12Let the field exult, and all that is in it; Then will all the trees of the forest sing for joy 13Before Yahweh; for he comes, For he comes to judge the earth: He will judge the world with righteousness, And the peoples with his truth.
