Language > English > Updated Bible Version > Psalms > Chapter 13

1For the Chief Musician. A Psalm of David. How long, O Yahweh? Will you forget me forever? How long will you hide your face from me? 2How long shall I take counsel in my soul, Having sorrow in my heart all the day? How long will my enemy be exalted over me? 3Consider [and] answer me, O Yahweh my God: Lighten my eyes, or else I will sleep the [sleep of] death; 4Or else my enemy will say, I have prevailed against him; [and] my adversaries will rejoice when I am moved. 5But I have trusted in your loving-kindness; My heart will rejoice in your salvation. 6I will sing to Yahweh, Because he has dealt bountifully with me.
