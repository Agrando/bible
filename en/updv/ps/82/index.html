Language > English > Updated Bible Version > Psalms > Chapter 82

1A Psalm of Asaph. God stands in the congregation of God; He judges among the gods. 2How long will you{+} judge unjustly, And respect the persons of the wicked? Selah. 3Judge the poor and fatherless: Do justice to the afflicted and destitute. 4Rescue the poor and needy: Deliver them out of the hand of the wicked. 5They don't know, neither do they understand; They walk to and fro in darkness: All the foundations of the earth are shaken. 6I said, You{+} are gods, And all of you{+} are sons of the Most High. 7Nevertheless you{+} will die like man, And fall like one of the princes. 8Arise, O God, judge the earth; For you will inherit all the nations.
