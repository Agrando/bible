Language > English > Updated Bible Version > Job > Chapter 25

1Then Bildad the Shuhite answered and said, 2Dominion and fear are with him; He makes peace in his high places. 3Is there any number of his armies? And on whom does not his ambush arise? 4How then can common man be just with God? Or how can he be clean who is born of a woman? 5Look, even the moon has no brightness, And the stars are not pure in his eyes: 6How much less is common man, who is a worm! And the son of man, who is a worm!
