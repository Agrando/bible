1Then Joshua called the Reubenites, and the Gadites, and the half-tribe of Manasseh, 2and said to them, Ye have kept all that Moses the servant of LORD commanded you, and have hearkened to my voice in all that I commanded you. 3Ye have not left your brothers these many days to this day, but have kept the charge of the commandment of LORD your God. 4And now LORD your God has given rest to your brothers, as he spoke to them. Therefore now turn ye, and get you to your tents, to the land of your possession, which Moses the servant of LORD gave you beyond the Jordan. 5Only take diligent heed to do the commandment and the law which Moses the servant of LORD commanded you, to love LORD your God, and to walk in all his ways, and to keep his commandments, and to cling to him, and to serve him with a 6So Joshua blessed them, and sent them away, and they went to their tents. 7Now to the one half-tribe of Manasseh Moses had given in Bashan, but to the other half Joshua gave among their brothers beyond the Jordan westward.
