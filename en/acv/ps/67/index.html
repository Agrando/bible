Language > English > A Conservative Version > Psalms > Chapter 67

1God be merciful to us, and bless us, [and] cause his face to shine upon us (Selah), 2that thy way may be known upon earth, thy salvation among all nations. 3Let the peoples praise thee, O God. Let all the peoples praise thee. 4O let the nations be glad and sing for joy. For thou will judge the peoples with equity, and govern the nations upon earth. Selah. 5Let the peoples praise thee, O God. Let all the peoples praise thee. 6The earth has yielded its increase. God, even our own God, will bless us. 7God will bless us, and all the ends of the earth shall fear him.
