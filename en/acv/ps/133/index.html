Language > English > A Conservative Version > Psalms > Chapter 133

1Behold, how good and how pleasant it is for brothers to dwell together in unity! 2It is like the precious oil upon the head, that ran down upon the beard, even Aaron's beard, that came down upon the skirt of his garments, 3like the dew of Hermon, that comes down upon the mountains of Zion. For there LORD commanded the blessing, even life for evermore.
