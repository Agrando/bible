Language > English > A Conservative Version > Psalms > Chapter 47

1O clap your hands, all ye peoples. Shout to God with the voice of triumph. 2For LORD Most High is awesome. He is a great King over all the earth. 3He subdues peoples under us, and nations under our feet. 4He chooses our inheritance for us, the glory of Jacob whom he loved. Selah. 5God has gone up with a shout, LORD with the sound of a trumpet. 6Sing praise to God, sing praises. Sing praises to our King, sing praises. 7For God is the King of all the earth. Sing ye praises with understanding. 8God reigns over the nations. God sits upon his holy throne. 9The rulers of the peoples are gathered together [with] the people of the God of Abraham. For the shields of the earth belong to God. He is greatly exalted.
