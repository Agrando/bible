Language > English > A Conservative Version > Psalms > Chapter 110

1LORD says to my lord, Sit thou at my right hand until I make thine enemies thy footstool. 2LORD will send forth the rod of thy strength out of Zion. Rule thou in the midst of thine enemies. 3Thy people offer themselves willingly in the day of thy power, in holy array. Out of the womb of the morning thou have the dew of thy youth. 4LORD has sworn, and will not repent. Thou are a priest forever after the order of Melchizedek. 5LORD at thy right hand will strike through kings in the day of his wrath. 6He will judge among the nations. He will fill with dead bodies. He will strike through the head in many countries. 7He will drink of the brook in the way, therefore he will lift up the head.
