Then I said, O my lord, what shall be the outcome of these things? 9And he said, Go thy way, Daniel, for the words are shut up and sealed till the time of the end. 10Many shall purify themselves, and make themselves white, and be refined, but the wicked shall do wickedly. And none of the wicked shall understand, but those who are wise shall understand. 11And from the time that the continual [burnt-offering] shall be taken away, and the abomination that makes desolate is set up, there shall be a thousand and two hundred and ninety days. 12Blessed is he who waits, and comes to the thousand three hundred and thirty-five days. 13But go thou thy way till the end is. For thou shall rest, and shall stand in thy lot at the end of the days.
