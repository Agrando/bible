Ye eat, but ye have not enough. Ye drink, but ye are not filled with drink. Ye clothe yourselves, but there is none warm. And he who earns wages earns wages [to put] into a bag with holes. 7Thus says LORD of hosts: Consider your ways. 8Go up to the mountain, and bring wood, and build the house. And I will take pleasure in it, and I will be glorified, says LORD. 9Ye looked for much, and, lo, it came to little, and when ye brought it home, I blew upon it. Why? says LORD of hosts.
