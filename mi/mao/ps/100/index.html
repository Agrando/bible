Language > Maori > Maori Version > Psalms > Chapter 100

1He himene whakamoemiti. Kia hari te hamama ki a Ihowa, e nga whenua katoa: 2Mahi atu ki a Ihowa i runga i te koa, waiata haere ki tona aroaro. 3Kia matau ki a Ihowa, ko ia te Atua: nana tatou i hanga, ehara hoki i a tatou: ko tana iwi tatou, ko ana hipi e hepara ai. 4Tomokia ona tatau i runga i te whakawhetai, ona marae i runga i te whakamoemiti: whakawhetai atu ki a ia, whakapaingia tona ingoa. 5He pai hoki a Ihowa, pumau tonu tana mahi tohu: a kei tena whakatupuranga, kei tena whakatupuranga, tona pono.
